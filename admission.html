ADMISSION REGULATIONS – B.P.E.S

Intake, Eligibility and Admission Procedure

Intake:

There shall be a basic unit of 60 (Sixty) students for each year.

Eligibility:

  • a) Qualification: A pass in +2 in the 10+2 pattern.
  • b) Age: Not completed 21 years as on 1st July. For SC / ST candidates 3 years relaxation is given.
  • c) Represented the School in any Sports / Games.
  • d) Medically fit without any deformity

Enclosure:

The applicant should attach the following certificates.

  1. Mark statements of 10th and 12th
  2. School representation certificate in a Sport/Game
  3. Transfer and Conduct Certificates
  4. Community Certificate
  5. Aadhaar Card
  6. Medical Fitness Certificate from a Government Medical Officer in the prescribed format. (Download from the College Website)

Selection Tests:

S.NO TEST MARKS
1 Qualifying Examination 25
2 Participation in games and sports 25
3 Games skill test 50
4 Track and Field skill test 50

Admission Procedure:

Admission shall be made on merit on the basis of marks obtained for 150 by following the Rule of Reservation of Govt of Tamilnadu.

ADMISSION REGULATIONS – B.P.Ed

Intake, Eligibility and Admission Procedure

Intake:

There shall be a basic unit of 100 (One Hundred) students with two sections of 50 (Fifty) each.

Eligibility:

  1. Qualification: Bachelor's Degree in any discipline in 10+2+3 years pattern with 50% of marks from a recognized University and having at least participated in the inter college/Inter Zonal/District/School competition in sports and Games as recognized by the AIU/IOA/SGFI/ Govt. of India.
  2. Bachelor's degree in physical education with 45% of marks
  3. Bachelor's degree in any discipline with 45% of marks and studied physical education as compulsory/elective subject
  4. Bachelor's degree with 45% of marks and having participated in National/Inter University/State Competitions or secured 1st, 2nd or 3rd position in Inter College/Inter-Zonal/District/School competition in sports and games as recognized by the AIU/IOA/SGFI/Govt. of India
  5. Bachelor's degree with participation in international competitions or secured 1st, 2nd, or 3rd position in National/Inter-University competition in sports and games as recognized by respective federations/ AIU/IOA/SGFI/Govt. of India
  6. Graduation with 45% marks and at least three years of teaching experience (for deputed in-service candidates i.e., trained physical education teachers/coaches)

b) Age: Not completed 30 years as on 1st July. For SC / ST 3 years relaxation is given

Enclosure:

The applicant should attach the following certificates.

  • Mark statements of 10th, 12th and UG Degree
  • School/College representation certificates in a Sports/Games
  • Transfer and Conduct certificates
  • Community certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from a Government Medical Officer in the prescribed format. (Download from the College Website)

Selection Tests:

Admission shall be made on the basis of ranking for a total of 150 marks as detailed below.

S.NO TEST MARKS
1 Qualifying Examination 20
2 Participation in games and sports 25
3 Games skill test 50
4 Track and Field skill test 50
5 Bonus marks for profession of basic degree in Physical Education 5
TOTAL 150

Admission Procedure

Admission shall be made by following the Rule of Reservation of Govt of Tamilnadu.

ADMISSION REGULATIONS – M.P.Ed

Intake, Eligibility and Admission Procedure

Intake

There shall be a basic unit of 40 (Forty) students for each year

Eligibility

  • a) Bachelor of Physical Education (B.P.Ed) or equivalent with atleast 50% of marks
  • b) Age not completed 35 years as on 1st July. For SC / ST 3 years relaxation is given
  • c) Should have participated in the minimum Intercollegiate Level Participation in Sports and Games.

Enclosure:

The applicant should attach the following certificates.

  • Mark statements of 10th, 12th, UG and B.P.Ed Degree
  • College representation certificates in a Sports/Games
  • Transfer and Conduct certificates
  • Community certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from a Government Medical Officer in the prescribed format. (Download from the College Website)

Selection Tests:

Admission shall be made on the basis of ranking for a total of 150 marks as detailed below.

S.NO TEST MARKS
1 Qualifying Examination 20
2 Participation in games and sports 25
3 Games skill test 50
4 Written test 35
5 Personal interview 15
TOTAL 150

Admission Procedure

Admission shall be made on merit by following the Rule of Reservation of Govt of Tamilnadu.

ADMISSION REGULATIONS- Ph.D (Part Time & Full Time)

Rules and Regulations for Ph.D

Minimum Qualification for Admission M.P.Ed. / M.P.E.S. / M.P.E

The candidates who secure 50% of the total marks altogether in entrance test, Interview and PG Degree are eligible for Ph.D. admission.

The candidates qualified in NET/SLET/SET and other National Level Eligibility Test are exempted from entrance test and they have to appear for the interview only. Such candidates secure less than 50% of marks in interview are not eligible for Ph.D. admission.

S.NO TEST MARKS
1 Entrance Test: General Paper on Teaching and Research Aptitude (50 Objective Type Questions) 50
2 Paper on Concerned Subjects (50 Objective Type Questions) 50
3 Interview 25
4 P.G Degree 25
TOTAL 150

Admission Procedure

Important Instructions to the Candidates

  • Application for the Ph.D programme can be downloaded from the university website, www.tnpesu.org.
  • A Demand Draft of Rs.750/- shall be drawn in favour of the "The Registrar, Tamil Nadu Physical Education and Sports University, Chennai -600127" should be submitted along with the filled-in application towards the cost of application for the Ph.D programme.

Admission Procedure for International Students

  • Application Form: International students must fill out the application form available on the college website (www.drsacpe.com). The form typically requires personal details, academic qualifications, and course preferences.
  • Required Documents: Applicants need to submit essential documents, including:
    • A valid passport
    • Academic transcripts and certificates (translated into English if necessary)
    • A statement of purpose
    • Passport-sized photographs
    • Any additional documents specified by the TNPESU for the chosen program
  • Eligibility Criteria: Each program has specific eligibility requirements. International students must ensure they meet the academic standards outlined for their desired course.
  • Admission Test/Interview: Some programs may require students to appear for an entrance exam and Selection Trails, depending on the course requirements.
  • Confirmation of Admission: Once the application is reviewed, successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter. Students must confirm their acceptance by paying the requisite fees.
  • Visa Application: After securing admission, students should apply for a student visa. Dr. Sivanthi Aditanar College of Physical Education, Tiruchendur, provides assistance in this process, guiding students on necessary documentation and procedures.
