About NSPE College

The prime objective of colleges is to make the student a cultivated individual imbued with the passion for meaningful knowledge and a sense of values. The training and development of the human soul, the liberation of the mind, the awakening of the consciousness of personal dignity and the dedication of one's service for the cause of human progress constitute the vital responsibility of higher education for the orderly growth and development of the student.

Programs Offered

B.P.E.S (3 Years)

A pass in +2 Examination in the 10+2 pattern Should have participated (minimum) in Inter School Competitions in any Game or Sport Should not have completed 21 years of age as on 1st July.
A relaxation of 3 years of age given for SC/ST candidates. Should be medically fit and free from any deformity.

B.P.Ed (2 Years)

Degree in any discipline in 10+2+3 pattern with minimum 50% of marks (from a recognized University) Should have participated in Inter-Collegiate/Inter Zone/District School competition in any Sport or Game recognized by AIU/IOA/SGFI/Govt of India. Should not have completed 30 years of age as on 1st July.
A relaxation of 3 years of age shall be given for SC/ST Candidates. Should be medically fit and free from any deformity

M.P.Ed (2 Years)

Bachelor Degree in Physical Education (B.P.Ed) or equivalent degree with minimum 50% of marks. Should have participated (minimum) in Inter-Collegiate level competition in any Sport or Game.
Should not have completed 35 years as on 1st July. A relaxation of 3 years of age shall be given for SC/ST Candidates.Should be medically fit and free from any deformity.

Ph.D (Full Time/Part Time)

As per Tamil Nadu Physical Education and Sports University norms.

Secretary

Sri La Sri Narayana Desiga Swamigal

Being a pillar of support in all our endeavours, Sri la Sri Narayana Gnana Desika Swamigal, the present 14 th Pontiff of Koviloor Mutt who is the Secretary of our college has been an active contributor to the growth of the institution in many ways. He developed a deep knowledge in equity investments and became a reputed investment analyst and enabled the investors to build strong equity portfolio.
He got associated with Koviloor Madalayam and Kamban Trust in 1997 and became a Trustee. He is also managing KambanKarpagam Matriculation Hr.Sec School run by the Kamban Trust. From then onwards he became a part of Koviloor Madalayam.
Correspondent

Dr.M.VEERAPPAN, MBA, CCA, M.Phil, Ph.D

Dr.M.Veerappan has got more than twenty five years of experience to his credit. Principal
